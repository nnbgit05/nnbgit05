## What I'm working on
- **[Cryptic](https://github.com/nnbgit05/cryptic)** — locally hosted password manager with secure authentication, PostgreSQL-backed storage, and Redis-based session management.
- **[Blackbot](https://github.com/nnbgit05/blackbot)** — chess engine built in Python/C++ that reached ~2100 strength and beat strong engines including Komodo 17 and Fairy Stockfish 6.
- **[Prosperity Scope](https://github.com/nnbgit05/prosperity-scope)** — browser-based trading analytics dashboard for the IMC Prosperity algorithmic trading challenge.
- **Neurosurgical risk analytics platform (in progress)** — co-building a risk and resource allocation platform for neurosurgery using real clinical data. Uses survival analysis, Monte Carlo simulation, logistic regression, and constrained optimisation with validated clinical scoring systems including POSSUM, Glasgow Coma Scale, Spetzler-Martin, and WHO tumour grading. Built in R with an interactive Shiny frontend alongside a medical partner specialising in neurosurgery.
- **Nuclear regulatory AI (in progress)** — co-designing a framework for applying LLMs to safety-critical document review in nuclear engineering without treating model outputs as inherently trustworthy. The architecture combines a deterministic requirements database, an LLM-driven semantic matching layer, and a deterministic completeness checker that verifies whether all relevant requirements have been addressed. Motivated by a partner’s experience with operational experience reviews at EDF Nuclear Services, the project aims to impose structure, traceability, and determinism on an otherwise probabilistic workflow.
